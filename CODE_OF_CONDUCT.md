## Code Of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free community.

## Expected Behavior

* Be respectful and professional.
* Give feedback that is specific and constructive.
* Assume positive intent and seek clarification before escalating.
* Focus discussions on the work, not individuals.

## Unacceptable Behavior

* Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
* Discriminatory or hateful language
* Repeated disruption of discussions, reviews, or community spaces
* Sharing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Repository maintainers may edit, remove, or reject content that violates this code of conduct and may limit participation when needed.

## Reporting

To report a concern, open a private security advisory or contact the maintainers through private channels listed in this repository.
